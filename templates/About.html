Who are we

We are a few data science enthusiasts who are trying to make solve the financial market using the power of artificial intelligence and data.


Our aim

Our aim is to make investment easy for beginners in the market by providing market sentiment and price prediction based on LSTM and BERT sentiment analysis. Our goal is to make fundamental and technical analysis of a stock easier, for now we are working with Google stock prices but we will add more in the future. To identify the fundamental strength of a stock, the Petroski F-Score and the market sentiment with the companies financial data is enough. On our platform you will get access to all of it in a great visual format which will make trading a lot easier for you in the short term as well as the long term


What we dont advice

We don't claim our algorithms to be 100% accurate, our algorithms are more of a confirmation indicator which can help you identify trends in the market. Always do your own research before investing in any financial market and risk only that much money which you can happily lose.
